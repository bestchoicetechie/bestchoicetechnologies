Best Choice TechEdu Program

Best Choice TechAcademy is an initiative to promote the acquisition of strong and reliable ICT knowledge and application at the early stage in students in secondary/form six colleges.

Students who attend schools participating in out tech Education program from Junior Secondary to Senior Secondary would have covered the approved secondary school computer science Curriculum and gain lots of practical knowledge as the program is design to focus the practical ICT Skil acquisition than the conventional ICT standard attainable in most Nigeria Schools.

The Program builds them to becoming professional ICT personels at the end of their secondary school education and they are mandated to build useful ICT learning tools like Apps, Websites and other forms of applications for their various schools.
